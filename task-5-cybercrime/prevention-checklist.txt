Part B – Prevention Checklist:


Stay Safe Online – Checklist
- Don’t share your OTP or UPI PIN with anyone 
- Always check links before clicking 
- Don’t trust unknown calls claiming to be from banks 
- Use strong passwords for your accounts 
- Enable two-factor authentication 
- Avoid sharing personal information on random websites 
- Keep your phone and apps updated regularly 
- Think before accepting any payment request 


 For UPI / financial safety:
 - Never approve unknown payment requests 
 - Always double-check the receiver before sending money 


 If any issues are faced:
- Report at: https://cybercrime.gov.in 
- Helpline: 1930
